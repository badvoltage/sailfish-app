Welcome to the Bad Voltage app for SailfishOS

This is a Bad Voltage client app for SailfishOS.

Current Features

  • Listing available Episodes from RSS
  • View details of one particular episode
  • Mark episodes as seen
  • Stream episode from internet
  • Download episode for offline listening
  • Cover actions to resume/pause playback and reload rss
  • Shortcut to the Bad Voltage Community Page

Download

You can download the app from OpenRepos or directly from Jolla store.
